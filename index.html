About Me

Hi there, I'm Anjali.


I'm a Michigan based digital nomad who lives and travels in my self converted Promaster Van and vintage boat. I explore with my two dogs Theodore and Remington and share my stories of solo travel, DIY building, and tiny living.


My goal is to inspire others to take on solo travel, vanlife, other unconventional lifestyles no matter how big (or tiny). I hope to build a community of like minded adventurers who support each others dreams, spread love, and are excited to share in this journey we're all on together.


Come follow along on my adventures!

Lets work together!

I'm always looking to collaborate with people and brands that align with my values and community. I want to provide my audience genuine recommendations for products that I believe in, and I love finding new stuff to bring to my followers.
