About Me

  • Engineering and Design

    I am a senior studying computer science engineering at the University of Michigan with a special interest in front end web development and UX design. I have explored these interests through my courses at Michigan, independently studying different languages and methods of design, and creating my own websites and apps. What I love most about front end development and UX design is that I get to influence what the user sees and how they interact with a product. I am always trying to find ways to incorperate design and development into projects that I am passionate about..

  • Aviation

    My desire to become a pilot was sparked after my freshman year of college, I applied for a job at an airport and fell in love with the community and the freedom of being an aviator. I am now a student at Kalitta Flight School and intend to earn all of my ratings until I am a certified airline pilot. Flight training has taught me to be diligent and pay attention to details, I have learned to be resourseful and think on my feet both in the air and on the ground. As someone who loves to travel and see the world from a different perspective, there is nothing I love more than being able to get into my Cessna and take to the skies.

  • Personal

    As a full time student of both engineering and aviation finding free time can be difficult. But I always take any opportunity I can to have fun and enjoy time with friends. Some of my favorite things to do are slackline, hike, run, and travel. I have been fortunate enough to find amazing people to share these experienes with who support me in whatever I do. I am also a musician- Having been classically trained in piano for 13 years and many other instruments throughout middle and high school, I love to get involved with music whenever possible in college. As a member of Groove High Energy Percussion I am able to express myself through music, enjoy the company of some dope people, and have a ton of fun collaborating and creating great shows each semester.
