About Me

  • Engineering

    I am a graduate of the University of Michigan with a BSE in computer science engineering, in September 2020 I will begin working at Amazon as a software development engineer in the Bay Area and I couldn't be more excited! My focus is in front end development and design and I have devoted much of my independent study outside of college classes to learning different languages and developing apps and websites. What I love most about front end development and UX design is that I get to influence what the user sees and how they interact with a product. I am always trying to find ways to incorperate design and development into projects that I am passionate about.

  • Aviation

    My desire to become a pilot was sparked after my freshman year of college, I applied for a job at an airport and fell in love with the community and the freedom of being an aviator. I am now a private pilot and intend to continue my training until I am a commercial pilot. Flight training has taught me to be diligent and pay attention to details, I have learned to be resourseful and think on my feet both in the air and on the ground. As someone who loves to travel and seeing the world from a different perspective, there is nothing I love more than being able to get into the cockpit take to the skies.

  • Personal

    When working a full time job from home it can be difficult to separate work from regular life, but I always take any opportunity I can to have fun and enjoy time with friends. Some of my favorite things to do are slackline, hike, and travel. I have been fortunate enough to find amazing people to share these experienes with who support me in whatever I do. I am also a musician- having been classically trained in piano for 13 years and many other instruments throughout middle and high school, I love to get involved with music whenever possible. Recently I have been traveling with my dog Theo in a van that I built out after college, I have had so many wonderful experiences from this chapter in my life and am always looking forward to the next one.
